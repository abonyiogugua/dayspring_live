🌅 About Dayspring Assembly

Welcome to Dayspring Assembly Where Light Breaks Forth!
At Dayspring Assembly, we are a vibrant, spirit-filled community
of believers committed to spreading the love, truth, and power of Jesus Christ.
Rooted in faith and led by the Holy Spirit.
Dayspring Assembly, you’ll find a home where purpose is discovered,
faith is strengthened, and destinies are fulfilled.

Our Core Pillars
  • Word of God:Teaching and living out biblical truth
  • Worship:Intimate and powerful worship experiences
  • Prayer:Creating a lifestyle of prayer and intercession
  • Discipleship:Raising believers to grow and serve in Christ
  • Community Impact: Reaching out with love, service, and support
Our Vision:

To be a light to the nations, a place where broken lives are restored,and God’s power is made manifest.
